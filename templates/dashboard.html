About DigiDoctor

DigiDoctor was created to help people in places they are unfamiliar with, such as vacation spots in South Florida, or a random amusement park. With this, we hope to bring some form of healthcare to everyone, whether through our unique Health Hax or connecting more serious patients with healthcare specialists.
